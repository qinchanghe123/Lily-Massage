Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your visit.

How early should I arrive before my appointment?
We recommend arriving 10-15 minutes prior to your scheduled appointment. This allows you time to check in, use the restroom, and communicate any specific needs or problem areas to your therapist without cutting into your massage time.
Can I get a massage if I have an injury or medical condition?
In many cases, massage is highly beneficial for recovery. However, if you have acute injuries, recent surgeries, or specific medical conditions (like severe osteoporosis or blood clots), please consult your doctor first. Always inform your therapist about any physical issues so they can adjust their techniques.
What is your cancellation policy?
We kindly ask for at least a 24-hour notice if you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment. This allows us to offer the slot to other clients. Last-minute cancellations or no-shows may be subject to a fee.
Is there convenient parking at the clinic?
Yes! We have plenty of free, accessible parking right in front of our building at 901 Cypress Creek Road. You won't have to stress about finding a spot.
Do I have to undress completely?
Your comfort is our top priority. You may undress to your level of comfort. Our therapists are trained in professional draping techniques, meaning you will always be modestly covered during the session, with only the area being worked on exposed.
